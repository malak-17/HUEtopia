About Us

HUETopia is an accessibility app designed to empower individuals with visual impairments or color blindness. With tools like color adjustments and traffic light detection, our goal is to make technology inclusive and user-friendly for everyone.

Key Features:

  • - Customization Colors: Adjust brightness, contrast, and saturation for better visibility.
  • - Camera Integration: Detects and displays real-time traffic light colors and others.
  • - Default Modes: Optimized for Red-Green and Blue-Yellow color blindness.
